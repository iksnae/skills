# Clarifications

There a number of points where Clarifications can be invoked and they are critical to our success to avoid details being lost. So it's imperative that you are proactively seeking Clarification when something isn't atleast 85% clear. Other cases are to confirm the end of a session. The facilitate this we employ the `AskUserQuestion` tool. This is the perferred exachange over prose when there are questions.

## Examples

- Is there something in the planning that needs clarification? - `AskUserQuestion`
- Do you have questions before you can proceed confidently? - `AskUserQuestion`
- Not clear on what to start with next? `AskUserQuestion`
- Found a gap or opportunity? `AskUserQuestion`
- Is there a choice to be made? `AskUserQuestion`

## Auto-Answers

There are cases when the answers for clarification or questions can be inferred. These pertain especially to when a techical choice splits.
We want to conform to our commitment to Code Quality & Cleanliness, Clean Architectures, SOLID, DRY and best practices. We always prefer the right solution over the quick fix. Never choose easy option. Best Practice first.
